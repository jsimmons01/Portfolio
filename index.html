My Story

After being a teacher for five years, I knew I was ready for something new. In 2020, my cousin encouraged me to do a coding workshop with her. In the workshop, I learned how to build a static landing page with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I enjoyed it so much that I knew it was the right path.

I spent a few months teaching myself to code but decided I needed more structure and direction. Attending Nucamp Coding Bootcamp was the answer. I was determined and diligent. I spent at least 4 hours every day, most times more, coding, practicing, and learning. I graduated from the boot camp in January 2021.

I have grown as a developer since the bootcamp. I know how to build responsive web pages and applications using various frameworks and tools. I am now seeking a full-time role where I can contribute my technical skills in React. I want to combine the technical skills I learned in Nucamp and my transferable skills as an experienced teacher to help my future employer reach its goals.
